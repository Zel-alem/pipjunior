Our Story

About Pip Junior

We're on a mission to give every child the financial education that schools forget to teach.

How It Started

Born from a Real Problem

Pip Junior Publishing was founded by parents who noticed something missing in their children's education — money skills. Kids were learning to read, write, and do maths, but nobody was teaching them about saving, earning, or the difference between needs and wants.

So we created Professor Pip — a wise, friendly owl who makes financial concepts feel like an adventure. Every book and worksheet is designed to spark curiosity and build habits that last a lifetime.

🦉

Professor Pip

The wise owl who believes every child has the potential to be a money hero — they just need the right guide.
